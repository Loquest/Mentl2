# Mentl - Your Mental Health Companion
## A Complete Guide to Your Mental Wellness Journey

---

## 🌟 What is Mentl?

**Mentl** is your personal mental health companion app designed to support you through every step of your mental wellness journey. Whether you're managing ADHD, depression, bipolar disorder, or OCD, Mentl provides the tools, insights, and support you need—24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Think of Mentl as having a supportive friend, a personal coach, and a wellness tracker all in one app. It learns about you, adapts to your needs, and helps you understand yourself better.

### Who is Mentl for?

**For You (If You're Managing a Mental Health Condition)**
- Track your moods and spot patterns you might miss
- Get instant support when you're struggling
- Build better habits with tools designed for how your brain works
- Understand your triggers and what helps you feel better

**For Your Support Circle (Caregivers)**
- Stay connected to loved ones you care about
- Receive alerts when someone needs extra support
- Understand patterns to provide better help
- Respect privacy while staying informed

## 📱 Core Features Explained

### 1. 📊 Mood & Symptom Tracking

**What it does:** Helps you log how you're feeling each day so you can spot patterns over time.

**How to use it:**
1. Tap "Log Mood" from the dashboard
2. Rate your mood on a scale of 1-10
3. Rate your energy level
4. Log how many hours you slept
5. Select any symptoms you're experiencing
6. Mark if you took your medication
7. Add any notes about your day

**Why it matters:** When you track consistently, you start to see connections. Maybe you notice your mood dips after poor sleep, or certain symptoms appear before difficult days. This awareness is powerful for managing your mental health.

**Pro tip:** Try to log at the same time each day—it only takes 30 seconds and becomes a helpful habit!

---

### 2. 🤖 AI Chat Assistant

**What it does:** Provides 24/7 support through an intelligent AI companion that understands mental health.

**How to use it:**
1. Tap "AI Chat" from the menu
2. Type whatever's on your mind
3. Get supportive responses, coping strategies, and guidance

**What you can talk about:**
- How you're feeling right now
- Challenges you're facing
- Need coping strategies for anxiety
- Want grounding techniques
- Just need someone to "listen"

**Safety features built in:**
- 🚨 **Crisis Detection**: If you express thoughts of self-harm, the app immediately provides emergency resources
- 📞 **Direct Hotline Links**: One tap to call 988 or Crisis Text Line
- 👥 **Caregiver Alerts**: With your permission, caregivers are notified during crisis moments

**Important:** While our AI is supportive and helpful, it's not a replacement for professional mental health care. Always reach out to a healthcare provider for medical advice.

---

### 3. 📈 Visual Analytics & Insights

**What it does:** Transforms your mood data into easy-to-understand charts and insights.

**Four powerful views:**

**Overview Tab**
- See your mood trend over time (is it improving, stable, or declining?)
- View mood distribution (what percentage of days were good vs. difficult?)
- Quick summary cards with key stats

**Patterns Tab**
- AI identifies patterns in your data
- See which days of the week tend to be better or harder
- Understand your weekly rhythm

**Triggers Tab**
- Discover what might be triggering difficult days
- See which symptoms appear most often
- Understand symptom relationships

**Correlations Tab**
- See how sleep affects your mood
- Understand if medication is helping
- Find connections you might have missed

**Export your data:** Download everything as a CSV file to share with your therapist or doctor.

---

### 4. 👥 Caregiver Network

**What it does:** Connects you with trusted family members or friends who want to support you.

**For the person being supported:**
1. Go to Caregivers page
2. Invite someone by their email
3. Choose what they can see:
   - View your mood logs
   - View your analytics

4. You can change or remove access anytime

**For caregivers:**
1. Accept the invitation
2. View the "People I Care For" section
3. Check in on mood trends
4. Receive alerts if a crisis is detected

**Privacy first:** You're always in control. Caregivers only see what you explicitly allow them to see.

---

### 5. 🍎 Nutrition Suggestions

**What it does:** Provides personalized food recommendations based on your mood and energy level.

**How it works:**
1. Set your dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
2. Add any allergies or intolerances
3. The AI suggests foods that may help your current mood state

**Three types of suggestions:**
- **Quick Snacks**: 5-minute mood-boosting options
- **Recipes**: Step-by-step meals with brain-healthy ingredients
- **Meal Plans**: Full day of eating for optimal mental wellness

**The science:** Certain foods can influence brain chemistry. For example:
- Omega-3s (fish, walnuts) support brain health
- Complex carbs help with serotonin production
- Protein provides building blocks for neurotransmitters

## 🧠 ADHD Tools Suite

*These features appear only if you've added ADHD to your conditions.*

### Tool 1: Task Chunking Engine

**The problem it solves:** Big tasks feel overwhelming and lead to procrastination.

**What it does:** Uses AI to break any task into small, manageable steps.

**How to use it:**
1. Go to Tools → Task Chunker
2. Enter your task (e.g., "Clean my room")
3. Click "Break into Steps"
4. AI creates 5-7 tiny steps, ordered from easiest to hardest

**Example transformation:**
- **Before:** "Write research paper" 😰
- **After:**
  1. Open a blank document and save it with a name (2 min)
  2. Write down the main topic in one sentence (3 min)
  3. List 3 things you already know about the topic (5 min)
  4. Find and bookmark 2 sources (10 min)
  5. Write the introduction paragraph (10 min)
  ...and so on

**Why it works:** The ADHD brain struggles with task initiation. When the first step is tiny and specific, it's much easier to start.

---

### Tool 2: Focus Session (Task + Timer Combined)

**The problem it solves:** You have the steps, but staying focused through them is hard.

**What it does:** Creates a guided, immersive focus experience that walks you through each chunk with built-in timers and breaks.

**How to use it:**
1. Create a task with chunks
2. Click "Start Focus Session"
3. The app shows you one chunk at a time
4. A timer counts down based on the chunk's estimated time
5. When done, a break automatically starts
6. After the break, the next chunk appears
7. Repeat until complete!

**Features:**
- 🔔 **Sound alerts** when phases end (so you don't have to watch the clock)
- 📳 **Vibration** on mobile devices
- 🔇 **Mute option** if you prefer silence
- ⏭️ **Skip** button if you finish early
- 🎉 **Celebration screen** when you complete all chunks!

---

### Tool 3: Adaptive Pomodoro Timer

**The problem it solves:** Traditional 25-minute timers don't work for everyone.

**What it does:** A flexible focus timer that adapts to what works for YOU.

**How it's different from regular Pomodoro:**
- Choose any duration (not stuck at 25 minutes)
- Tracks your success at different durations
- Suggests your optimal focus length based on history
- Records focus quality ratings so you learn what works

**How to use it:**
1. Go to Tools → Focus Timer
2. Set your desired focus duration
3. Optionally name what you're working on
4. Press play and focus!
5. Rate your focus quality when done

---

### Tool 4: Dopamine Menu

**The problem it solves:** When transitioning between tasks, ADHD brains often fall into "doom scrolling" or unhealthy avoidance patterns.

**What it does:** Gives you a personalized menu of quick, healthy dopamine boosts.

**Pre-loaded options:**
| Time | Examples |
|------|----------|
| **1-2 min (Micro)** | Stretch, drink water, look out window |
| **5-10 min (Short)** | Listen to a song, text a friend, doodle |
| **15-30 min (Medium)** | Walk around the block, watch funny videos |
| **30+ min (Reward)** | Bigger rewards for completing major tasks |

**How to use it:**
1. Finished a task? Need a break?
2. Go to Dopamine Menu
3. Choose a category based on how much time you have
4. Or click "Surprise Me!" for a random suggestion
5. Do the activity, then get back to work refreshed

**Customize it:** Add your own activities that bring you joy!

---

### Tool 5: Time Blindness Guard

**The problem it solves:** People with ADHD often struggle to estimate how long things take.

**What it does:** Tracks your time estimates vs. actual time to improve awareness.

**What you'll see:**
- Your accuracy percentage
- Recent tasks with estimated vs. actual comparison
- Tips for improving time estimation
- Total focus time tracked

**Why it helps:** By seeing data on your time perception, you start to naturally calibrate better. Maybe you learn "I always underestimate by 50%" and can adjust accordingly.

---

### Tool 6: Energy-Aware Scheduling

**The problem it solves:** Trying to do demanding work during low-energy times sets you up for failure.

**What it does:** Analyzes your mood and focus patterns to identify your peak hours.

**What you'll see:**
- Your best hours for demanding work
- Visual chart of energy throughout the day
- Personalized recommendation (e.g., "Your peak is 9-11 AM")
- Low-energy times (good for easier tasks)

**How it works:** The app analyzes:
- When you log good moods and high energy
- When your focus sessions are most successful
- Patterns across days and weeks

---

### Tool 7: Rewards Center

**The problem it solves:** ADHD brains need immediate rewards to stay motivated.

**What it does:** Gamifies your productivity with XP, levels, streaks, and badges.

**Earn rewards by:**
- Completing tasks (+10 XP)
- Finishing focus sessions (+5 XP)
- Crushing chunks (+2 XP each)
- Maintaining daily streaks

**Badges you can earn:**
- 🚀 **First Step** - Complete your first task
- 🏆 **Task Master** - 10 tasks completed
- 👑 **Task Champion** - 50 tasks completed
- 🔥 **Focus Warrior** - 10 focus sessions
- ⚡ **Focus Master** - 50 focus sessions
- 🔥 **On Fire** - 3-day streak
- ⭐ **Weekly Warrior** - 7-day streak
- 🏅 **Unstoppable** - 30-day streak
- ⏰ **Hour of Power** - 1 hour total focus
- 🎖️ **Focus Legend** - 10 hours total focus

## 🆘 Crisis Support

**Mentl takes your safety seriously.** If you express thoughts of self-harm or suicide, the app will:

1. **Immediately show crisis resources**
2. **Provide one-tap calling** to:
   - 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
   - Crisis Text Line (text HOME to 741741)
3. **Alert your caregivers** (if you've enabled this)

**Remember:** You are not alone. Crisis feelings are temporary, and help is available 24/7.

## 💡 Tips for Getting the Most from Mentl

### For Everyone:
1. **Log daily** - Even just mood and energy takes 10 seconds
2. **Be honest** - The more accurate your data, the better your insights
3. **Review weekly** - Check your analytics every Sunday to spot trends
4. **Use the AI chat** - It's always there when you need to process feelings

### For ADHD Users:
1. **Start small** - Begin with the Task Chunker on ONE task
2. **Use Focus Sessions** - The guided experience is game-changing
3. **Check your energy patterns** - Schedule important work during peak hours
4. **Reward yourself** - Watch those badges pile up!

### For Caregivers:
1. **Respect boundaries** - Only view what your loved one has shared
2. **Respond to alerts promptly** - Crisis notifications are important
3. **Use insights constructively** - Share observations supportively, not critically
4. **Celebrate progress** - Acknowledge when things are going well
